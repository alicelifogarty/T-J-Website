Our Story

Soon after they met in 2012, Tiantian and Jay began dating. She worked at State Street, and he would always bring her breakfast. They loved spending time together, going on endless trips and adventures.

Time and time again, circumstances would put distance between them, but Jay always found a way back to Tiantian. When her OPT time limit was up and she returned to China in 2013, he would fly to Shanghai to see her. In 2014, when Tiantian moved to New York for a month, Jay would drive four hours to get there each weekend.

Long distance (especially long distance between countries) proved difficult, and they amicably went their separate ways.

Then in November 2015, Tiantian invited Jay to a concert in her hometown. He flew all the way from Boston to Shanghai. It was the first time they met after one year and two months apart. They hugged tightly and knew they never wanted to be apart again.
